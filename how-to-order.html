How to order?

For your convenience, we have made the procedure of ordering goods as simpler and quicker as possible! You just need to take a few simple steps:

Make an order on our website.

Wait for the operator to call for details of the order.

After your confirmation, we will send out your order the same day.

You get the parcel and pay.

  1. Go Back and choose the product you need.
  2. Enter your Name and Phone Number.
  3. Wait for the operator to call you back.

Any questions left?

To clarify other details on the ordering of goods in the Sodafoods store, you can contact our operator by phone. It is absolutely free. To do this, just enter your Name and Phone Number on the page of the product you selected. The operator will contact you shortly.
